ABOUT

Kia Ora!

My name is Emese Peter and I am a second year Creative Media Production student at Massey University Wellington.

Growing up, I was always fascinated by the idea of ‘movie magic’ and have been drawn to watching behind the scenes clips of movies and tv shows. I really adored the visual experience of watching movies and knew I wanted to be a part of that one day. By chance in 2018 I ended up getting roped into taking part in the 48 hour film festival and from there I knew there was no going back.

My passion for movies and the creative industries has led me down the path of creating media and I wouldn’t have it any other way.
